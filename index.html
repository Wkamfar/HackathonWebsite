What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon where people come together to build cool projects, learn new skills, and collaborate intensively, usually over a short period like 24-48 hours, but in this case it is only from 8:30-5:00. There are workshops where people can hone desired skills and food is provided for all participating.

It's a great place to bring ideas to life, meet like-minded individuals, and push your creative and technical boundaries.
